Red Bull were no strangers to F1 - as sponsors - prior to formally entering as a works team in 2004. Nonetheless, the scale of their success over the following decade was staggering. After a first podium in 2006, the team hit their stride in 2009 quadruple champion. Now they are recapturing that glory with an equally exciting talent – one named Max Verstappen…
